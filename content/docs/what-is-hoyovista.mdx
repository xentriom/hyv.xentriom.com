## Overview

HoYoVista is a comprehensive Discord bot that brings HoYoLAB functionality directly to your Discord server. Whether you want to automate daily check-ins, view your game profiles, track your income, or redeem codes automatically, HoYoVista has you covered.

## Key Features

### Daily Check-ins

Automatically claim your daily rewards from HoYoLAB for all your linked games. Set up automatic check-ins that run daily, or manually check in whenever you want.

### Profile Viewing

View detailed game profiles including:

- Character information and stats
- Achievement progress
- Spiral Abyss progress (Genshin Impact)
- Battle trial progress (Zenless Zone Zero)
- And much more!

### Real-Time Notes

Keep track of your in-game resources in real-time:

- **Genshin Impact**: Resin, daily commissions, realm currency, parametric transformer
- **Honkai: Star Rail**: Trailblazer powder, daily training, simulated universe
- **Zenless Zone Zero**: Battery charge, daily missions, season missions
- **Honkai Impact 3rd**: Stamina, Q-Manifold, memorial arena

### Income Tracking

View detailed monthly income reports showing:

- Primogems/Polychromes earned
- Breakdown by source (commissions, events, etc.)
- Comparison with previous months
- Daily earnings summary

### Automatic Code Redemption

Automatically redeem codes for your linked games. The bot fetches available codes from external APIs and redeems them automatically based on your settings.

### Privacy & Security

- Privacy blur option to hide sensitive information
- Secure cookie storage
- Optional data collection for analytics
- Full control over your data

## Getting Started

1. Use `/register` to create your account
2. Use `/hoyolink` to link your HoYoLAB account
3. Configure your settings with `/settings`
4. Start using commands like `/check-in`, `/profile`, `/notes`, and more!

## Privacy & Data

HoYoVista respects your privacy. You can:

- Control what data is collected
- View all your stored data with `/data`
- Delete your account and data at any time
- Enable privacy blur to hide sensitive information

For more information, see our [Privacy Policy](/docs/privacy-policy) and [Terms of Service](/docs/terms-of-service).
