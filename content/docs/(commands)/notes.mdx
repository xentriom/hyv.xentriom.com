## Notes Command

The `/notes` command displays real-time information about your in-game resources, including stamina, daily missions, weekly activities, and more.

### Usage

```
/notes account:<game>
```

### Parameters

- **account** (required): The game account to view notes for. Use autocomplete to select from your linked games.

### Supported Games

All major HoYoverse titles are supported:

- Genshin Impact
- Honkai: Star Rail
- Honkai Impact 3rd
- Zenless Zone Zero

### Genshin Impact Notes

Displays:

- **Original Resin**: Current/max resin and time until full
- **Enemies of Note**: Remaining resin cost-halving opportunities this week
- **Daily Commission Reward**:
  - Daily commission completion status
  - Encounter points status
  - Long-term encounter points stored
- **Jar of Riches**: Realm currency and time until limit
- **Parametric Transformer**: Cooldown status and days until available

### Honkai: Star Rail Notes

Displays:

- **Trailblazer Powder**: Current/max stamina and restoration time
- **Daily Training**: Current/max training score
- **Echo of War**: Weekly attempts used/available
- **Simulated Universe Points**: Current/max points
- **Currency War Points**: Current/max weekly points

### Honkai Impact 3rd Notes

Displays:

- **Stamina**: Current/max stamina and restoration time
- **Daily BP**: Current/max training score
- **Q-Manifold** or **Superstring Dimension**:
  - Time left or status
  - Current score and tier
- **Memorial Arena**:
  - Time left or lock status
  - Challenge rewards progress
  - SSS-rank rewards progress
- **Elysian Realm**: Time left and current score

### Zenless Zone Zero Notes

Displays:

- **Battery Charge**: Current/max energy and restoration time
- **Daily Missions**:
  - Engagement today
  - Scratch Card Mania status
  - Video Store Management status
- **Season Missions**:
  - Bounty Commission progress
  - Ridu Weekly Points
  - Refresh times

### Real-Time Updates

- All data is fetched in real-time from HoYoLAB
- Times are displayed using Discord's relative time format
- Data updates every time you run the command

### Requirements

- Must be registered (`/register`)
- Must have linked your HoYoLAB account (`/hoyolink`)
- Must have at least one linked game account
