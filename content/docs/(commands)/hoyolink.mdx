## HoYoLink Command

The `/hoyolink` command allows you to link or unlink your HoYoLAB account with HoYoVista. This is required for most bot features to work.

### Usage

```
/hoyolink
```

### What It Does

- Links your HoYoLAB account by storing your cookies securely
- Automatically detects and links all your game accounts
- Allows you to unlink your account if needed

### Getting Your Cookies

To link your account, you'll need your HoYoLAB cookies. The command includes a built-in guide, or you can follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step>Go to [HoYoLAB](https://www.hoyolab.com/home) and log in</Step>
  <Step>Click your profile picture → **"Personal Homepage"**</Step>
  <Step>Open browser DevTools (F12 or right-click → Inspect)</Step>
  <Step>Go to the **"Network"** tab</Step>
  <Step>Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)</Step>
  <Step>Filter for **"getGame"** and click **"getGameRecordCard"**</Step>
  <Step>Under **"Request Headers"**, copy everything after **"Cookie:"**</Step>
  <Step>Paste into the modal when linking</Step>
</Steps>

### Required Cookies

The following cookies are required:

- `ltmidV2`
- `ltokenV2`
- `ltuidV2`

### After Linking

Once linked, you'll see a list of recommended commands:

- `/profile` - View statistics for your games
- `/income` - View income for the selected month
- `/roadmap` - View planned improvements and features
- `/settings` - Access and modify your account settings

### Unlinking

To unlink your account:

1. Use `/hoyolink` again
2. Click **"Unlink HoYoLAB Account"**
3. Confirm the action

**Warning:** Unlinking will delete all your linked game data and cookies. This action cannot be reversed.

### Security

- Cookies are stored securely in the database
- Only you can access your linked account
- You can unlink at any time to remove your data

### Troubleshooting

**"No games found linked to this account"**

- Ensure your HoYoLAB account has at least one game linked
- Try refreshing your cookies

**"Invalid or incomplete cookies"**

- Make sure you copied the entire cookie string
- Check that all required cookies are present
- Try getting fresh cookies from HoYoLAB

**"Error fetching game data"**

- Your cookies may have expired
- Try getting fresh cookies and linking again
