## Register Command

The `/register` command is the first step to using HoYoVista. It creates your account and allows you to start using all the bot's features.

### Usage

```
/register
```

### What It Does

- Creates your HoYoVista account
- Links your Discord account to the bot
- Sets up default settings for your account

### After Registration

Once registered, you'll see a list of recommended commands:

- `/hoyolink` - Link/unlink your HoYoLAB account
- `/settings` - Access and modify your account settings
- `/data` - Manage your data, including viewing or deleting it

### Privacy

By registering, you agree to our [Privacy Policy](/docs/privacy-policy) and [Terms of Service](/docs/terms-of-service).

### Notes

- You only need to register once
- If you're already registered, the command will inform you
- Registration is required before linking your HoYoLAB account
