## Income Command

The `/income` command displays detailed monthly income reports showing how many resources you've earned in-game, broken down by source.

### Usage

```
/income account:<game> month:<month>
```

### Parameters

- **account** (required): The game account to view income for. Use autocomplete to select from your linked games.
- **month** (required): The month to view income for. Shows the last 3 months by default.

### Supported Games

- **Genshin Impact**: Full income tracking
- **Zenless Zone Zero**: Full income tracking
- Honkai: Star Rail and Honkai Impact 3rd are not currently supported

### Genshin Impact Income Report

Displays:

- **Primogems**: Total earned this month
- **Mora**: Total earned this month
- **Breakdown by Source**:
  - Commissions
  - Events
  - Spiral Abyss
  - And more
- **Daily Summary**: Today's earnings (if current month)
- **Comparison**: Percentage change from previous month
- **Primary Source**: Your main source of Primogems this month

### Zenless Zone Zero Income Report

Displays:

- **Polychromes**: Total earned this month
- **Master Tapes**: Total earned this month
- **Boopons**: Total earned this month
- **Breakdown by Source**:
  - Various revenue streams
- **Primary Source**: Your main source of Polychromes this month

### Data Delay

- **Genshin Impact**: Data is delayed by approximately 1 hour
- **Zenless Zone Zero**: Data is delayed by approximately 2 hours
- The diary only includes resources obtained outside of top-ups

### Month Selection

The month autocomplete shows the last 3 months:

- Current month
- Previous month
- Month before that
